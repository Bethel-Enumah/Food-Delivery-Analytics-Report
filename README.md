# Food Delivery Analytics

## Project Overview

This project analyzes food delivery operations to uncover performance bottlenecks, customer service issues, and revenue drivers. By examining order timestamps, driver activity, restaurant performance, delivery locations, discounts, tips, and refunds, the analysis identifies where delays occur, measures operational efficiency, and evaluates overall business profitability.

The goal is to generate actionable insights that support faster deliveries, improved customer experience, and better business decision-making.



## Business Problem

Food delivery companies often face challenges such as delayed deliveries, inefficient driver allocation, restaurant preparation delays, and revenue losses caused by excessive discounts and refunds.

This project aims to answer:

* Are restaurants or drivers the major source of delays?
* Which delivery areas experience the slowest service?
* How do discounts, tips, and refunds affect profitability?


## Dataset

The dataset contains order-level operational and financial information, including:

* Date
* Time customer placed order
* Time order placed at restaurant
* Time driver arrived at restaurant
* Delivered time
* Driver ID
* Driver name
* Restaurant ID
* Customer ID
* Delivery area
* ASAP
* Sub total
* Delivery fee
* Service fee
* Discount
* Tip
* Refunded amount



## Key Metrics

The analysis focuses on the following KPIs:

* Average delivery time
* On-time delivery rate
* Restaurant preparation time
* Driver wait time
* Delivery travel time
* Total revenue
* Net revenue
* Average order value
* Total discounts
* Total refunds
* Refund rate
* Tip rate

## Key Insights Explored

* Delivery performance across different delivery areas
* Peak demand periods and order patterns
* Driver efficiency and delivery speed
* Restaurant preparation performance
* Refund trends and possible service issues
* Financial impact of discounts and service fees
* ASAP order performance compared with regular orders



## Tools Used

* **Power BI** — data cleaning, modeling, dashboard design, and visualization
* **DAX** — calculated measures and KPI creation



## Expected Outcome

The project provides data-driven recommendations that can help optimize delivery operations, reduce delays, improve customer satisfaction, and support better pricing and resource allocation decisions.
